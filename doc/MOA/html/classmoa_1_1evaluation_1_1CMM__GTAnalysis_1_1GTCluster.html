ArrayList<Integer> moa.evaluation.CMM_GTAnalysis.GTCluster.clusterRepresentations = new ArrayList<Integer>() [private]

a new GT cluster consists of one or more "old" GT clusters.

Connected/overlapping clusters cannot be merged directly because of the underlying cluster model. E.g. for merging two spherical clusters the new cluster sphere can cover a lot more space then two separate smaller spheres. To keep the original coverage we need to keep the orignal clusters and merge them on an abstract level.
